Verification Scope

We check everything. Not just "active or not."

Most eligibility tools only confirm that a patient's policy is active. That's not enough. An active plan with a $6,000 unmet deductible or a missing referral can still generate a denial — or an unhappy patient who didn't expect the bill. We verify the whole picture.

Active coverage confirmation

Policy effective dates, termination dates, and plan type — confirmed against real-time payer data, not cached batch files.

Deductible & out-of-pocket

Current YTD spend toward deductible and out-of-pocket max — so your front desk can collect the right amount at check-in.

Co-pay & co-insurance

Exact co-pay amounts per visit type (PCP, specialist, labs, imaging) so patients are never surprised by a bill they expected you to absorb.

Referral & auth requirements

Is a referral required from the PCP? Does this procedure need prior auth? Flagged 48 hours in advance with automatic auth queue handoff if needed.

COB & Medicare secondary

Coordination of benefits for patients with dual coverage, Medicare secondary payer rules, and Medicaid crossover — checked and sequenced correctly before billing.

Patient notification included

If there's a significant out-of-pocket balance, we generate a patient-facing summary your front desk can share at check-in. Reduces billing disputes by 60%.

Before vs. After

What happens when you verify before every visit.

The math is simple: catching a $400 deductible issue before the patient arrives is worth $0. Catching it after they've received care means a collections problem, a bad review, and often a write-off. We move that catch upstream.
