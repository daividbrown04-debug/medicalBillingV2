What We Believe

Three principles we refuse to compromise

Radical transparency

If you can't see it, you can't trust it. Every claim, every adjustment, every fee we charge is visible in ClearView™, 24/7. We built the dashboard first and the company around it.

  • Real-time claim status — no waiting for reports
  • Itemized fee breakdown every month
  • Denial root-cause analysis, not just counts

Aligned incentives

Percentage-of-collections pricing, contingency-only A/R recovery, and a written performance guarantee. We only do well when you do well — structurally, not rhetorically.

  • Performance guarantee in writing from day one
  • A/R recovery priced on contingency only
  • No monthly fees during setup or transitions

Real humans, reachable

Your account manager knows your providers by name and answers in hours, not ticket queues. US-based, AAPC/AHIMA certified, and paid on your outcomes — not their throughput.

  • Dedicated AM assigned before day one
  • Average response time: under 2 hours CT business hours
  • Weekly check-ins for the first 90 days

Our Culture

We hire for obsession, not credentials.

The best medical billing team in the US isn't the one with the most certifications — it's the one that cares the most about getting it right. We hire people who lose sleep over a dollar that didn't make it to a provider.
