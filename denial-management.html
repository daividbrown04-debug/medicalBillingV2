DenialShield™ Two-Layer Defense

Prevent first. Pursue everything else.

Layer 1: Pre-submission Prevention

Before every claim leaves your system, DenialShield™ cross-checks it against 8 years of payer-specific denial patterns. Coding conflicts, missing modifiers, auth gaps, and bundling issues are caught and corrected before submission.

  • CPT/ICD-10 mismatch detection by payer
  • Modifier validation against payer LCD/NCD rules
  • Prior auth requirement pre-check
  • Bundling / unbundling analysis
  • Timely filing window monitoring

Layer 2: 100% Appeals Queue

No denial is silently written off. Every denied claim enters our managed appeals queue — worked within 5 business days, escalated to peer-to-peer when clinically indicated, and reported to you weekly with status and dollar amounts.

  • 100% of denials in appeals queue — no exceptions
  • Priority-tiered by dollar value and appeal deadline
  • Peer-to-peer escalation for medical necessity
  • State external review coordination
  • Write-off requires your explicit sign-off — always

CO-16~24% of denials

Claim lacks information

Missing modifier, incomplete diagnosis, or admin data error. Pre-submission scrub catches these before they leave the system.

CO-97~18% of denials

Service already adjudicated

Duplicate claim or line item already paid/denied. Claim deduplication at charge capture prevents most.

CO-4~14% of denials

Inconsistent with procedure

CPT-ICD mismatch — the diagnosis doesn't support the procedure billed. Specialty code pairing library catches these.

CO-50~12% of denials

Medical necessity not met

Payer doesn't believe the service was necessary. Documentation review + peer-to-peer appeal if denied.

CO-109~9% of denials

Not covered by plan

Usually an eligibility issue — wrong plan billed. Real-time eligibility verification 48 hrs before every visit prevents these.

CO-29~8% of denials
