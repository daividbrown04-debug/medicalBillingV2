Why Specialty Matters

General billing knowledge is the most expensive kind.

Wrong modifiers cost you

A misplaced modifier 59 vs. XE on a bundled procedure triggers a denial that can take 90 days to appeal — and may never get paid if timely filing lapses.

Specialty-specific reimbursement

Cardiology payers have different bundling rules than orthopedic payers. Behavioral health has carve-outs. Generic coders miss these distinctions every day.

Audit risk varies by specialty

Pain management and oncology are among the highest-audit-risk specialties in CMS's target list. Coders who know these areas flag compliance risks before RAC auditors do.
