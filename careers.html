Our Culture

We built what we wish had existed when we started.

Remote-first, always

We've been remote since day one — not as a pandemic accommodation, as a deliberate design choice. The best coders and account managers don't all live in the same city. We hire the best humans wherever they are.

We pay for your certifications

CPC, CPC-H, COC, CPMA, CCS — we pay 100% of exam fees and study materials. We also give you paid study time during work hours. Your credentials help you and help our clients. That's the deal.

Performance bonuses tied to client success

When our clients' net collection rate goes up, you share in that. Quarterly bonuses are tied to client retention and collection rate improvements — not arbitrary metrics or call volume quotas.

Real specialization, real accountability

You're not a generalist. You're assigned to a specialty and a client portfolio — and you stay with that portfolio. We don't rotate coders or account managers across random clients. You know your accounts, and your accounts know you.

Sustainable workload, period

The medical billing industry is notorious for unsustainable caseloads. We cap coder portfolios, enforce reasonable client-to-AM ratios, and actually enforce work-life boundaries. Burnout is bad for our team and bad for our clients. We manage for the long game.

Senior Medical Coder — Cardiology

Full-time Remote — US $68K–$88K

We're looking for an AAPC-certified coder with 3+ years of cardiology-specific coding experience — echo, cath lab, EP, nuclear, device procedures. You'll be assigned to a portfolio of 3–5 cardiology group accounts and own the coding quality for those clients.

  • CPC required; CPC-H or COC preferred
  • Epic, athenahealth, or Kareo experience
  • Comfortable working payer-specific bundling rules
  • Strong knowledge of modifier 51, 59, XE/XS/XU/XP
